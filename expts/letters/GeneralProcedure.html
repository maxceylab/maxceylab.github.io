This experiment is a memorization task involving visual images. It will take approximately 30 - 45 minutes to complete.

Do not complete this experiment more than once, and please refrain from prolonged breaks once the experiment begins.


Next (1 of 2)

You will be presented with 60 letters, one at a time, each presented for 5 seconds.

Memorize each letter AND its font. You will later be tested on your memory for the studied letters. If you only memorize the letter but not its font, you will not do well on the test. Some letters may be presented in more than one font.

Take a minute to study all the fonts that will be used throughout the experiment, using the letters A and Z as examples. The letters A and Z will not be used for the actual experiment. We also provide a visual descriptor of each font in parentheses to aid as a mnemonic device.
